# Cookie Company Dashboard

![Cookie Company Dashboard](./Cookie%20Company%20Dashboard.png)

## Overview

The Cookie Company Dashboard is an executive and analytical Power BI dashboard designed to provide insights into sales performance, customer behavior, operational trends, and revenue growth.

This project combines high-level KPI monitoring with detailed analytical visuals, enabling users to identify business trends, evaluate customer performance, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive filtering and time-based analysis.

---

## Features

* 📊 Executive KPI tracking
* 🍪 Revenue and order trend analysis
* 👥 Customer performance insights
* 📈 Time-series business analytics
* 🕒 Interactive year-based slicer
* 🎯 Executive + analytical hybrid dashboard design
* 📦 Operational and sales performance reporting

## Key Business Insights

### Shrinking Average Order Value (AOV)

By analyzing the revenue and total orders trend lines, the dashboard reveals that Average Order Value (AOV) is gradually shrinking over time.

Initially, total revenue significantly outpaced total orders. However, as the timeline progresses, the two trend lines begin converging, suggesting that although order volume continues growing, revenue per order is decreasing.

This insight could indicate:

* Smaller average purchase sizes
* Increased discounting or lower-priced products
* Changing customer purchasing behavior

This trend highlights a potential opportunity to improve upselling, bundling strategies, or premium product marketing.

---

### High-Value Client Analysis

The Top Clients by Revenue visual highlights the company’s most valuable customers and identifies opportunities for relationship expansion.

The dashboard indicates that top-performing clients such as:

* Guzman Group
* Hoff Holdings
* Davidson LLC

generate a disproportionately large share of total revenue.

Based on this insight, the company could strengthen long-term profitability by:

* Expanding targeted marketing efforts
* Building loyalty or partnership programs
* Increasing account management focus
* Offering tailored promotions or enterprise pricing

Maintaining strong relationships with high-value customers can significantly improve revenue stability and long-term business growth.
